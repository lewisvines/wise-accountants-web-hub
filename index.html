Why the same template can't ship three times

the regulatory clock per market
France

A hard cliff

1 Sept 2026: every business must receive e-invoices; large & mid-sized firms must issue and e-report. 1 Sept 2027: SMEs and micro join. Issued via an Approved Platform (PA) — which is what AKAO is — into the public portal (PPF).

Single, dated deadline
Spain

Two clocks at once

VeriFactu (certified, tamper-evident billing software) rolls through 2026–27. Crea y Crece B2B e-invoicing — Royal Decree approved Mar 2026 — bites for large firms ~2027 and everyone else ~2028. Two obligations, one platform answer.

Dual obligation
Portugal

Layered, already running

No B2B clearance mandate. Certified software + ATCUD + QR + monthly SAF-T have been live since 2022–23. "2027" is really just the much-deferred QES-on-PDF point; SAF-T Accounting slips to 2028. The pressure is real but it isn't a cliff.

Continuous, deferred
